Define Your WHO

Your WHO is how you show up at your best — before goals, roles, or titles.

This guided assessment helps you identify:

  • Values — your non-negotiables
  • Pillars — your best-self characteristics
  • Ideal Emotion — your daily compass
  • Trigger — the inner story that pulls you off course

How it works

  1. Values (Discover)
    Use real moments (proud + upset) to surface what truly matters to you.
  2. Values (Road Test)
    If someone violates it, does it evoke emotion? YES = Value. NO = move to Pillars.
  3. Pillars
    Identify the characteristics you embody when you’re happiest and most “you.”
  4. Ideal Emotion
    Choose the feeling that guides your decisions — and how strongly you want it (1–10).
  5. Trigger
    Name your loud “I’m not ___” story and create a simple plan to respond.
